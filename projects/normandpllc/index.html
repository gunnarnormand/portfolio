Meet our team!

Senior Partner Edmund Normand, Normand PLLC. more_vert

ed@ednormand.com

Senior Partner Edmund Normand, Normand PLLC. close

Ed Normand started his practice in 1991 and has specialized in personal injury law. Early in his career, Ed Normand gained valuable experience at his first job working under the supervision of the late Al Cone, a founder of the Florida Justice Association and one of the foundational trial lawyers in the history of Florida Civil Trial Law. Later in 1991 Ed was offered a position at Wooten, Honeywell and Kest, a law firm with long history of trial excellence in Orlando, Florida. Ed is now the founder and partner in Normand Law PLLC.

Attorney Jacob Phillips, Normand PLLC. more_vert

jacob@ednormand.com

Attorney Jacob Phillips, Normand PLLC. close

Jacob Phillips is an attorney at Normand PLLC. He acquired his B.A. in History from the University of Central Florida and his J.D. from the University of Florida Levin School of Law. While in law school, he clerked for Judge Eric Melgren, Federal District of Kansas. He also interned for the Ninth Circuit Office of the Public Defender. Since graduating, he has worked on a large consumer protection class-action lawsuit, as well as cases involving personal injury, immigration, and family law. He is passionate about protecting those in need.

Attorney Alex Couch, Normand PLLC. more_vert

alex@ednormand.com

Attorney Alex Couch, Normand PLLC. close

Alex Couch is an Orlando-based attorney and businessman who has helped grow businesses in a plethora of industries, from construction to IT to film to law. He received his B.A. in History from the University of Central Florida and his J.D. from Florida A&M University School of Law. He has helped clients in a variety of legal practices, including class-action, personal injury, business development and copyright/trademark law. He is interested in continuing to develop his skills and experience in both consumer protection and transactional law.
