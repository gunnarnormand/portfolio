Beekeepers are seeing their colonies rapidly fall in past years. Instead of dwelling on this incredible tragedy, we decided to create a solution so everyone can help to save our domestic honeybee populations.

You may not be able to accomplish planting a whole garden, but even just one or two can make a huge difference and together we can save the bees! Your efforts will be appreciated by beekeepers, honeybees, flowers, trees, farmers, and honey lovers across the country.

we help put food on your tables, help us have food to eat..

For bees to carry out the pollination process, they need lots of nectar. Making your own bee-friendly garden is one way we can all help save the bees! Not only will the bees thank you, but your flowers will too!

Running low on space for your bee garden? You only need a small bit of land! Even window containers and rooftops create an inviting oasis for bees. Every little bit can help to nurture bees and other pollinators.

Spring is in the air, and so are the honey bees! Planting an herb garden will give bees nectar to eat and provide seasoning for your kitchen.

Still feel like you dont have the space to create your own bee oasis? That's okay. You can still help out the bees by donating to our cause. Every donation will go towards planting bee gardens, creating bee hives, and educating youth.

No donation is too small or large. We are here to create an environment for bees to buzz around and have plenty of food and homes to enjoy.
